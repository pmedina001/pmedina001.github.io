About the Project

This capstone project was developed by Pedro Medina as part of the MS in Data Science program with a focus in Artificial Intelligence at Florida International University. The project applies machine learning and geospatial data analysis to predict crime hotspots across Florida counties.

By integrating historical crime data with socio-economic indicators such as poverty, employment, education, and housing, the project identifies patterns that can inform proactive safety measures. The work is supervised by Dr. Levente Juhasz of Florida International University and aims to showcase how AI models, combined with spatial insights, can enhance crime prediction and policy planning.
